Virat Kohli

Virat Kohli is an international cricketer who currently captains the India national team. He was ranked eighth on ESPN's list of world's most famous athletes in 2016. Born in Delhi, Kohli shot to fame as the Under-19 skipper, who led India to victory at the 2008 Junior World Cup in Malaysia.He made his Test debut in 2011 and was part of the World Cup 2011 winning team.
